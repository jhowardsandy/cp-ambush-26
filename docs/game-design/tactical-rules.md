# Tactical Rules Status

Accepted rules currently cover timeline ordering, grid movement/occupancy, terrain movement cost, objective terrain line of sight, present-time faction visibility, deterministic vitality effects, guaranteed-hit direct attacks, encounter carry-forward, eliminate-all-opponents outcomes, named map areas, unit archetypes, and faction roster validation. Combat accuracy, cover, reactions, elevation, memory/fog of war, injury modeling beyond vitality/incapacity, and morale remain intentionally unspecified until their dedicated milestones.
