# Combat System

The direct-fire combat slice is implemented. `Attack` names an opposing `TargetUnitId` and data-defined `AttackProfileId`. At completion, `AttackRules` checks inclusive Manhattan range and required terrain line of sight against the current positions. A legal attack then draws one 1–100 value from the resolver's seeded random stream and hits when that value is no greater than `AttackProfile.AccuracyPercent`; a miss completes with zero vitality change. A hit applies cover-mitigated damage through the generic vitality effect calculation. `AttackResolved` exposes structured attacker/target positions and a human-readable accuracy, roll, result, cover, and applied-damage breakdown for presentation/replay; an illegal-at-resolution attack emits `ActionFailed`.

The starter service rifle and marksman rifle currently use provisional 75% and 85% accuracy values. Profiles default to 100% when the content omits accuracy, which keeps teaching fixtures deterministic. An attack profile may separately name ammunition and a positive ammunition cost; the resolver reserves planned attack/overwatch ammo and consumes it only for a legal direct-fire or triggered reaction attempt, independently of hit/miss. Cover, reaction fire, and ammunition are implemented; armor, evasion, facing, projectile travel, area effects, and concealment modifiers are deferred. `Aim` remains timing-only. See `../rulebook/direct-attacks.md` and `effect-system.md`.
